### Ingredients
- 3 cups packed finely chopped shredded kale (4 ½ ounces)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Kosher salt, to taste
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt, to taste
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes, optional
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more as needed
- 1/2 pound loose spicy Italian sausage (or sweet Italian or chicken sausage)
- 2 large carrots, small-diced (about 2 cups)
- 1/2 large yellow onion, small-diced (about 1 cup)
- 1 celery stalk, small-diced (about ½ cup)
- 28 ounce can crushed tomatoes
- 3/4 cup canned full-fat coconut milk (about half of a well-shaken 13.5-ounce can)
- 14 ounces gluten-free brown rice spaghetti (or rice and corn based pasta or regular pasta)

### Method
1. Make the crispy kale: Preheat the oven to 325°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. Combine the kale, olive oil, and a few pinches of salt in a large bowl and toss with tongs or hands until the kale is evenly coated and transfer to the prepared sheet pan.
3. Bake until dry and crispy, 25 to 30 minutes. Set aside.
4. Meanwhile, make the spaghetti and sauce: Combine the garlic powder, oregano, 1 teaspoon kosher salt, the black pepper, and pepper flakes (if using) in a small bowl and set aside.
5. Line a plate with paper towels and set near the stove. Heat the olive oil in a large, deep-lidded skillet over medium heat until shimmering. Add the sausage and cook, stirring occasionally to break up any large pieces, until browned, 5 to 6 minutes. Use a slotted spoon to transfer to the paper towels and set aside.
6. Heat the same skillet, adding a drizzle of olive oil if the pan is too dry, over medium heat. Add the carrots, onion, and celery and cook, stirring occasionally, until very soft, 10 to 12 minutes. Add the reserved spice mixture and cook, continuing to stir occasionally, until fragrant, 3 to 4 minutes. Stir in the crushed tomatoes. Bring to a simmer and cook until slightly reduced and warm, about 5 minutes. Stir in the coconut milk and cook until heated through, about 1 minute. Cover and set aside.
7. Make the spaghetti: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high heat. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente according to package directions. Drain in a colander. If using gluten-free pasta, rinse with cold water to prevent sticking (if using regular pasta, skip this step). Return to the pot and set aside.
8. In a blender or food processor, transfer the cooked vegetables and blend (with the steam vent in the blender top open) on high speed until completely smooth, about 1 minute. If you are using a blender smaller than 6 cups, you may need to do this in batches.
9. Mix half of the sauce with the cooked pasta until fully coated. Add more sauce if desired. Stir in the cooked sausage until evenly combined.
10. Spoon the pasta into four bowls and top with the crispy kale.
