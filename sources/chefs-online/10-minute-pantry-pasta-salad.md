### Ingredients
- 12 ounces red lentil or brown rice pasta, cooked according to package instructions and cooled (I love the Trader Joe’s red lentil penne)
- 15 ounces chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup roasted red peppers, thinly sliced
- 3/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1/2 cup pitted kalamata olives, halved
- 1/4 cup roughly chopped fresh parsley
- 3 green onions, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

### Method
1. Add the cooked and cooled pasta to a large mixing bowl. Add the chickpeas, roasted red peppers, feta, olives, parsley, and green onions. Toss to evenly distribute.
2. Add the olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, oregano, salt, garlic powder, and black pepper. Toss again to coat all ingredients in the dressing.
3. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
