### Ingredients
- 1 lb chicken breast
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1 avocado
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes, optional
- 15 oz can corn, drained and rinsed
- 15 oz can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 16 oz cauliflower rice, cooked (can sub for cooked white rice)
- 3 cups baby spinach
- 3 green onions, finely chopped
- 1 lime, quartered
- Hot sauce, for topping (optional)

### Method
1. Preheat the oven to 400F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Add the chicken to a large bowl. Toss with the salt, ground black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, and a generous drizzle of olive oil. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165F. Cool the chicken on the pan, then shred with a fork, kitchen shears, or an electric mixer.
3. In a large bowl, mash the avocado with the salt, ground black pepper, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes (optional). Add a squeeze of lime juice if desired.
4. To assemble the bowls, add the cooked cauliflower rice to the bottom of 4 bowls. Layer on the spinach, shredded chicken, black beans, corn, and top with a generous scoop of the avocado mash. Garnish with the green onion, a squeeze of lime, and hot sauce (if desired).
