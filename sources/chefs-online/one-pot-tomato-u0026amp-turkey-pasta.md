### Ingredients
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 yellow onion, diced (can sub 1 shallot)
- 1 lb ground turkey
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
- 32 oz tomato sauce
- 2 cups chicken broth (can sub water)
- 3 cups dry pasta of choice (I like penne for this one)
- for topping (optional): fresh chopped basil, lemon zest, u0026 ricotta cheese

### Method
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat and sauté the diced onion until translucent, about 5 minutes.
2. Add in the ground turkey and brown it all over, then add the garlic and spices, mix to combine, and sauté for 3-5 more minutes.
3. Pour in the tomato sauce, broth, & pasta. Stir to combine well & make sure the pasta is fully covered. Add a bit of water to fully cover if needed.
4. Cover the pot and cook on medium low heat for 14-18 minutes, until the pasta is al dente. Stir 2-3 times as it cooks to prevent any sticking.
5. Stir, then serve immediately or store in an airtight container in the fridge for 3-4 days.
