### Ingredients
- 1 spaghetti squash + olive oil, salt, u0026 black pepper for coating
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 4-6 garlic cloves, minced
- 1+ 1/2 cups chopped broccoli florets
- 12-16 jumbo shrimp, shells removed
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 1/2 cup fresh chopped basil, for topping

### Method
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. Slice your spaghetti squash in half and remove the seeds (it's easier to cut in half after stabbing with a fork a few times and then microwave for five minutes). Drizzle with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Place face down on the sheet pan and bake for 35-40 minutes, until tender to a fork.
3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat, then add the garlic. Saute for 1-2 minutes, then add the broccoli, shrimp, salt, and black pepper.
4. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side to fully cook. Add the lemon juice and cook down for another 1-2 minutes.
5. When the spaghetti squash is done cooking, use a fork to scoop out the insides (you can leave it inside the squash to eat out of or transfer to bowls). It should come out looking like your favorite spaghetti.
6. Top the spaghetti squash with even servings of the shrimp and broccoli mixture, plus fresh chopped basil leaves.
