### Ingredients
- 12 -15 medium frozen shrimp, de-veined
- 2 tbsp olive or avocado oil
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 inch piece ginger root, minced
- 1/2 broccoli head, chopped into 1-inch florets
- 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
- 4 baby bella mushrooms, chopped
- 1 jalapeno pepper, de-seeded u0026 chopped
- 2/3 cup shredded carrots (about 1 medium carrot)
- 2 green onions, chopped
- 16 oz frozen cauliflower rice (can use fresh)
- 1/4 cup coconut aminos or reduced sodium soy sauce
- 3/4 cup raw cashews
- lime juice (optional) for serving

### Method
1. If using frozen shrimp, run under room temp water until slightly defrosted then add the shrimp to a large bowl and toss with the oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat, then add the seasoned shrimp and cook for about 3 minutes, flip and cook for another 3-4 minutes. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
3. In the same skillet over medium heat, add the minced ginger and cook until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes. Add the chopped broccoli, bell pepper, mushrooms, jalapeño, carrots, and green onions and cook until softend, about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add the cauliflower rice and cook until fully defrosted, about 7 minutes. Pour in the tamari and stir to combine, then remove the skillet from the heat.
4. Add the cashews and toss to combine. Transfer even servings to plates and top with a squeeze of lime.
