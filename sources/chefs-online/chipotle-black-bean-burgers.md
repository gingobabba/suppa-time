### Ingredients
- 1 yellow onion, chopped
- 3 minced garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp avocado oil
- salt
- black pepper
- 1 smoked paprika
- 1 chili powder
- 1 cumin
- 1/4 cayenne pepper
- 2 eggs beaten
- 2/3 cup corn
- 1/4 cup green chiles
- 3 chopped chipotles in adobo (with sauce) (make sure these are gf if you don't eat gluten)
- 1/3 cup almond flour
- 3 and 1/2 cups black beans

### Method
1. sauté the onion + garlic until browning in the avocado oil, the mix in all the spices.
2. in a mixing bowl, add the beaten eggs, onion + garlic mixture, corn, chiles, chipotles + almond flour and combine.
3. add in the black beans and combine, then use a fork to smash the black beans, leaving some intact depending on the texture you prefer.
4. shape into ~10 burgers and bake on 325 for 25 min, flipping halfway through.
5. store in the fridge or freezer until enjoying! ☺️
