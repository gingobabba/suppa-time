### Ingredients
- 1 ripe mango, 1/4-inch dice
- 1 ripe avocado, 1/4-inch dice
- 1 English cucumber, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup fresh chopped cilantro
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes, optional
- 2 pounds salmon, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 1/3 cup coconut aminos or reduced sodium soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon sriracha
- 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 head butter lettuce
- 4 green onions, thinly sliced
- sesame seeds, for garnish

### Method
1. For the Mango Salsa
2. Combine the mango, avocado, cucumber, cilantro, lime juice, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes in a medium bowl. Chill in the refrigerator while you make the salmon.
3. For the Salmon Lettuce Wraps
4. In a large bowl, combine the cubed salmon with the coconut aminos, sriracha, sesame oil, maple syrup, ginger, salt, ground black pepper, and garlic powder. Coat all of the salmon in the sauce and let marinate for at least 10 minutes.
5. Preheat your air fryer to 400F. Place the salmon pieces on the air fryer pan and cook for 5 to 10 minutes, to your desired doneness. If you do not have an air fryer, heat the oven to 400F and bake on a sheet pan lined with parchment paper for about 10 minutes, to your desired doneness.
6. To assemble the wraps, divide the lettuce leaves among 4 plates. Add the salmon cubes, a spoonful of mango salsa, and chopped green onions to each. Garnish with sesame seeds, if desired.
