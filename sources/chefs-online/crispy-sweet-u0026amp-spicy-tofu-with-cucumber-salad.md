### Ingredients
- 1/2 english cucumber, thinly sliced
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 3 tbsp toasted sesame oil (can sub olive oil)
- 2 tbsp coconut aminos or reduced sodium soy sauce
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp ground ginger
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds (optional)
- 1 lb block of tofu, pressed for 30 min and cubed
- 1 tbsp tapioca flour, arrowroot starch, or cornstarch
- 1 tbsp coconut aminos or soy sauce
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp ground ginger
- 1 tbsp olive or avocado oil
- 2 tbsp sriracha
- 2 tbsp coconut aminos or reduced sodium soy sauce
- 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp pure maple syrup
- 1 tsp ground ginger
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds (optional)
- kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste

### Method
1. Combine all ingredients for the cucumber salad in a medium bowl. Mix to evenly combine, cover, and refrigerate until serving.
2. Preheat the oven 400F and line a sheet pan with parchment paper. Coat the cubed tofu in the starch, coconut aminos, spices, and oil. Spread in one even layer on the sheet pan and bake for 20 minutes, giving the pan a shake halfway through. (Option to air fry on 400F for 15 minutes)
3. Meanwhile, make the sweet and spicy sauce sauce: combine all the ingredients in a jar and shake, or whisk vigorously in a small bowl for 60 seconds.
4. Cover the baked tofu in the sauce (save a little for topping it you’d like), then continue to bake for 10 minutes, until crisp. (Option to air fry on 400F for 5 minutes)
5. Evenly divide the tofu and chilled cucumber salad among 4 plates, and enjoy.
