### Ingredients
- ground turkey: 1 pound
- shredded white cheddar cheese: ½ cup
- mayonnaise: ¼ cup
- garlic powder: 1 teaspoon
- chili powder: 1 teaspoon
- scallions: 4, thinly sliced
- mayonnaise (for sauce): ½ cup
- hot sauce: 2 tablespoons
- lime: 1
- iceberg lettuce: 1 head, thinly sliced
- neutral oil: 1 tablespoon
- burger buns: 4
- tomato: optional, thinly sliced
- red onion: optional, thinly sliced

### Method
1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Combine turkey, cheddar, mayo, garlic powder, chili powder, salt, and scallions in a bowl. Mix well and form into 4 thin patties, about ½ inch thick.
3. Make the sauce: stir together mayo, hot sauce, a pinch of salt, and lime juice. Set aside.
4. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add oil and swirl to coat. Cook patties without touching, 4–5 minutes per side until golden brown all over.
5. When you flip the burgers, place buns on a baking sheet and warm in the oven for 4–5 minutes.
6. Spread sauce on both sides of each bun. Add a patty and desired toppings.
7. Toss the sliced iceberg with ¼ cup burger sauce and a pinch of salt. Serve alongside.

### Notes
Can be grilled on medium-high, 3–4 minutes per side.
