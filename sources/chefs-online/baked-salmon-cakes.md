### Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons ghee or avocado oil
- 1 yellow onion, finely chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 14 ounces canned salmon or baked salmon filet
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon celery salt
- 2 tablespoons parsley
- 3 tablespoons coconut aminos or reduced sodium soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1/4 cup almond flour

### Method
1. Preheat oven to 375F nd line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. Heat the ghee in a skillet over medium-high heat and add the chopped onion and garlic. Cook until aromatic and browning, about 5 minutes. Remove from the hear and transfer to a large mixing bowl.
3. Add the salmon, all of the spices, coconut aminos, dijon mustard, beaten eggs and almond flour to the bowl and use clean hands to fully incorporate the mix. It should be wet but firm enough to shape.
4. Use a large cookie scoop or clean hands to form the salmon cakes into even circles and place them on the sheet pan. The mix should make about 8 large or 16 small salmon cakes.
5. Bake for 20-24 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean and the outsides are browned, then remove from oven and cool on the pan for about 5 minutes before serving.
