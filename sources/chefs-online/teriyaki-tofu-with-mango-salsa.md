### Ingredients
- 1/2 small red onion, chopped
- 1 ripe mango, cubed
- 1/2 avocado, pitted and cubed
- 1/3 cup fresh chopped cilantro
- juice of 1 lime
- kosher salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes to taste
- 14 ounces firm tofu, pressed*
- 1 tablespoon tapioca flour, arrowroot starch, or cornstarch
- 1/3 cup coconut aminos
- 1/3 cup water
- 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
- 2 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
- 1 big garlic clove, minced
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
- 1 cup basmati or jasmine rice, cooked according to package instructions

### Method
1. Make the mango salsa: combine all the ingredients for the salsa in a medium bowl. Chill until serving.
2. Make the sauce: combine all the ingredients in a medium bowl.
3. Cut the pressed tofu into 1/2-inch cubes and add to a large bowl. Toss to coat with 1 tbsp of starch. Pour the sauce in the bowl and let sit for at least 10 mins.
4. Heat a large skillet over medium heat with a tablespoon of oil. Add the tofu and sauce, and cook, stir constantly for 10-15 minutes, until the sauce is a thickened.
5. Serve over cooked rice, and top with the mango salsa.
