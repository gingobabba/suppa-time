### Ingredients
- 5 oz pre-cooked salmon (I used Chicken of the Sea Wild Caught Pink Salmon Packet)
- 12 oz cooked pasta of choice (I used gluten-free farfalle)
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 cup cooked asparagus (or you can use pickled asparagus)
- 3 cups fresh arugula
- 1/3 cup fresh chopped basil
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- 1/4 cup white wine vinegar (can sub apple cider or balsamic vinegar)
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 cup pesto

### Method
1. Simply add all ingredients to a large bowl and mix well. Chill until serving and store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.
