### Ingredients
- 1 pound ground lamb, 85% lean
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon avocado oil
- 8 butter or romaine lettuce leaves (or burger buns)
- sliced tomato, pickled red onion, pickles, crumbled feta cheese, diced tomatoes, for serving (optional)

### Method
1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground lamb, lemon zest, coriander, cumin, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Use clean hands to fully incorporate the spices into the lamb.
2. Using a large cookie scoop or clean hands, form the lamb into four even flattened circles and set aside.
3. Heat the avocado oil on a large skillet over medium-high heat, then carefully add each of the lamb burgers (cook in batches if needed).
4. Cook the burgers for about five minutes, then flip to cook the other side for another 5 to 8 minutes. Use a meat thermometer to monitor the internal temperature and remove them from the skillet when the internal temperature reaches 160F.
5. Immediately place the cooked lamb burgers into the lettuce wraps or buns and top with toppings of choice.
