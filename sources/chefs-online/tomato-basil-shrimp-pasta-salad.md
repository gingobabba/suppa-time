### Ingredients
- 1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined (41-50 count), thawed if frozen
- kosher salt
- ground black pepper
- 12 oz rotini pasta, cooked according to package instructions and cooled (I love Trader Joe's brown rice rotini)
- 3 cups fresh arugula
- 1 1/2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
- 3/4 cup fresh chopped basil leaves
- 3/4 cup crumbled feta
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes

### Method
1. Cook the shrimp: heat a large skillet over medium heat and spray with a light coating of oil spray. Toss the shrimp with a few pinches of salt and pepper in a mixing bowl, then add to the skillet in a single layer. Cook until lightly browned and opaque in the middle, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Set aside.
2. Add the cooked and cooled pasta to a large mixing bowl and add the arugula, tomatoes, basil, feta, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, lemon juice, salt, black pepper, oregano, and red pepper flakes. Toss to evenly mix.
3. Add the cooked shrimp and gently toss again. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
