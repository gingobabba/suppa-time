### Ingredients
- 2 sticks of butter, softened
- 4-5 minced garlic cloves
- tbsp fresh rosemary, finely chopped
- 2 tbsp fresh sage, finely chopped
- 2 tbsp fresh thyme, finely chopped
- 1 tsp sea salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

### Method
1. For best results, start by seasoning the entire turkey generously with salt and pepper.
2. Put the turkey to the side and prepare the compound butter by mixing the ingredients together.
3. Cover the turkey with your compound butter and cook in the oven. The general guidance is to cook the turkey for 13 minutes per pound at 350°F for an unstuffed turkey. Make sure the turkey is cooked to 165°F all the way through.
4. Carve and enjoy!
