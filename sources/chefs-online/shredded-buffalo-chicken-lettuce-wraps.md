### Ingredients
- 2 pounds chicken breast
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
- 1/4 cup ghee or butter, melted
- 1 head butter lettuce, separated
- 1/3 cup green onions, chopped
- 1/3 cup shredded red cabbage
- 1/4 cup ranch dressing

### Method
1. Preheat the oven to 400F and line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. Cover the chicken breasts in the salt, black pepper, and garlic powder, then place on the sheet pan and bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until at an internal temperature of 165F. Set aside to cool.
3. While the chicken cools, mix together the buffalo sauce and melted butter or ghee in a small bowl to make the sauce. Salt to taste if desired.
4. Use two forks or an electric mixer to shred up all of the cooked chicken. Once shredded, pour the sauce on top and mix to coat all of the chicken in the sauce.
5. To assemble the wraps, carefully spoon in the shredded buffalo chicken into the wraps, then top with cabbage, green onions, ranch dressing or any other desired toppings.
