### Ingredients
- 12oz pasta of choice, cooked + 1 cup reserved pasta water
- 3/4 cup cashews, soaked at least 3 hours or overnight
- 3/4 cup sundried tomatoes in olive oil
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 + 1/4 cups unsweetened almond milk
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tbsp nutritional yeast

### Method
1. Begin by cooking the pasta according to the instructions on the box in boiling salted water and reserve one cup of pasta water before draining.
2. While the pasta cooks, add the soaked cashews, sun-dried tomatoes, tomato paste, garlic, almond milk, spices, and nutritional yeast to a blender.
3. Blend everything on high speed until smooth, or until it can't be blended any further. Slowly begin incorporating the reserved pasta water until the sauce is smooth and blending easily. You may only need about half of the water depending on how thin you'd like the sauce.
4. When the sauce is at your desired consistency, pour it over the cooked pasta and mix until all of the pasta is completely coated. Serve immediately for best results.
5. If you don't use all of the sauce, store in a jar in the refrigerator for up to five days and reheat in the microwave.
