### Ingredients
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
- 2 pounds chuck roast
- kosher salt
- ground black pepper
- 1 yellow onion, diced
- 1 cup low-sodium beef broth
- 1/4 cup tomato paste
- Juice of 2 limes
- butter lettuce leaves, for serving
- thinly sliced radishes, for serving
- finely chopped cilantro, for garnish

### Method
1. In a small bowl, combine the ground cumin, garlic powder, paprika, ground coriander, cayenne pepper, and chili powder. Set aside.
2. Cover the chuck roast with salt and black pepper.
3. Heat a cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add the roast and sear each side for 3 to 4 minutes. Remove from the skillet and add to the slow cooker.
4. To the slow cooker, add the yellow onion, beef broth, tomato paste, lime juice, and the spice mix to the slow cooker. Stir to combine, cover, and cook on high for 4 hours or low for 8 hours.
5. Remove the chuck roast from the slow cooker and shred the beef with two forks. Add the shredded beef back to the slow cooker and stir to combine.
6. Divide evenly among lettuce cups (or tortillas). Top each with thinly sliced radish and garnish with cilantro.
