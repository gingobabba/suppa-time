### Ingredients
- 2-3 olive oil
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 3-4 white anchovy filets
- 28 oz crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
- 2/3 cup pitted u0026 halved black olives
- 3 tbsp capers
- salt, black pepper, u0026 red pepper flakes to taste
- 1 lb cooked pasta of choice (use gf pasta to keep it gluten-free)

### Method
1. In a large pan, add the olive oil and bring to a light simmer over medium heat. Add in the garlic and anchovies.
2. Use a spoon to break the anchovies apart and stir everything to combine. simmer for about 5 minutes.
3. When the garlic is toasty, add in the crushed tomatoes and stir. Season with salt and pepper as desired. simmer, stirring every few minutes, for ~12 minutes.
4. Add in the black olives, capers, and red pepper flakes to taste (omit for no spice). Stir to combine and simmer for another 5-10 minutes. season again to taste.
5. Add in the cooked pasta, coat with the sauce, and serve immediately for best results. Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days.
