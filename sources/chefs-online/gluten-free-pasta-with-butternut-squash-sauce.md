### Ingredients
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, finely diced
- 2 -3 celery ribs, finely chopped
- 2 large carrots, finely chopped
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/2 butternut squash, peeled, seeded, and diced into small cubes
- 2 tsp salt (+ additional to taste)
- 1/2 tsp black pepper (+ additional to taste)
- 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, finely chopped (or 1 tsp dried)
- 1 tbsp fresh thyme, finely chopped (or 1 tsp dried)
- 1 lb ground chicken
- 28 oz crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup white wine or chicken broth
- 2/3 cup heavy cream or coconut cream
- grated parmesan and chopped parsley, for serving
- cooked pasta of your choice (see below for the GF one)
- 400 g gluten-free flour
- 240 g eggs (4 large or 6 medium eggs)
- 30 ml olive oil

### Method
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add in the onion, celery, and carrots and cook for 7-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the onions are translucent.
2. Add the garlic and butternut squash and cook, stirring occasionally for 15-20 minutes, until the squash cubes are tender. Mix in the salt, pepper, rosemary, and thyme and sauté for 3 minutes. Add the wine or broth, stirring with a wooden spoon to scrape up any vegetables on the bottom of the pan, and bring to a simmer for 5 mins.
3. Add in the ground chicken, breaking up any large chunks and cook until browned, about 8 to 10 minutes.
4. Add the crushed tomatoes, stir, and simmer for at least 10 mins to allow the flavors to incorporate.
5. Stir in the cream. Serve over cooked pasta of choice with chopped parsley and parmesan (if desired) on top!
6. For the GF pasta
7. Combine all ingredients in a bowl, or by well method, and knead until combined & smooth
8. Roll out the dough with a rolling pin or with pasta machine to preferred thickness and cut out preferred pasta shape
9. Place the pasta in a pot of boiling water for 1 to 2 minutes, drain, and serve.
