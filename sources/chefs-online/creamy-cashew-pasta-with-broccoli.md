### Ingredients
- 1 1/4 cups raw cashews
- 12 oz brown rice pasta (or pasta of choice)
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
- 2 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 cup low sodium chicken or vegetable broth
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
- 1 cup chopped broccoli florets (about 1/3 of a head of broccoli)
- 1/3 cup fresh parsley, chopped

### Method
1. Add the cashews to a medium bowl or glass jar. Fill the bowl with hot water until all the cashews are covered. Set aside to soak for 30 minutes.
2. While the cashews soak, bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes. After 5 minutes, add the chopped broccoli to the pot with the pasta to steam. Continue to simmer for 3 minutes, then drain and set aside in the large pot.
3. Add the soaked cashews, garlic, salt, ground black pepper, Italian seasoning, chicken broth, lemon juice, and nutritional yeast to a food processor or blender. Blend on high speed until completely smooth, about 60 seconds. If the sauce is too thick, ad water 2 tablespoons at a time to thin.
4. Pour the sauce over the cooked pasta and broccoli and stir to evenly combine. To serve, top with chopped parsley and enjoy.
