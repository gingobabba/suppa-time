### Ingredients
- 1 large sweet potato, 1/2-inch cubes
- 1/2 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 1 broccoli head, chopped into 1-inch florets
- 6 oz baby bella mushrooms, sliced
- 1 package italian chicken sausages (5 pre-cooked sausages), sliced
- 2 tbsp olive or avocado oil
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
- 1 tbsp italian seasoning
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- 2 tsp garlic powder

### Method
1. Preheat the oven to 400F and line a large sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. Add the sweet potato, broccoli, mushrooms, and sliced sausages to the pan in one even layer.
3. Toss everything in the oil, salt, ground black pepper, italian seasoning, paprika, and garlic powder until evenly coated. Bake for 20-35 minutes, until the veggies are tender and the sausages have crisped edges.
4. Divide the vegetables and sausage evenly among 4 bowls to serve. Option to top with a red sauce, or other preferred sauce.
