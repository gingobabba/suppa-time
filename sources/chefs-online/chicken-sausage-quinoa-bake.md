### Ingredients
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, grated or minced
- 2 teaspoons dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
- 3 cups low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
- 4 ounces baby spinach, finely chopped (5 cups)
- 1 cup dry white or tricolor quinoa
- 1 pint cherry tomatoes
- 4 ounce feta block
- 12 ounces precooked chicken sausage, thinly sliced (I recommend Italian-style)
- juice of 1 lemon, additional wedges for serving if desired
- chopped parsley, for topping (optional)

### Method
1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Prepare a 9x13-inch baking dish.
2. Add the olive oil, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and red pepper flakes (if using) to the baking dish and whisk to combine.
3. Add the broth, spinach, quinoa, and cherry tomatoes to the baking dish and mix to evenly disperse. Place the feta block in the center of the dish and disperse the sliced chicken sausage around the sides of the feta.
4. Bake until the quinoa is cooked, feta is softened, and cherry tomatoes are beginning to burst, 32 to 36 minutes. Let sit on the counter for 5 minutes to cool slightly.
5. Use a wooden spoon to press down on the feta and mix it well with the rest of the components in the baking dish as it melts. Add the lemon juice and mix again.
6. Serve between four bowls or divide into four containers and top with chopped parsley (if using). Serve with a lemon wedge on the side if desired.
