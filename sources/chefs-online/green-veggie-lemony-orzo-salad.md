### Ingredients
- 8 ounces gluten-free orzo
- 3 cups asparagus, 1/4-inch dice
- 1 medium zucchini, 1/4-inch dice
- 1 shallot, finely diced
- 1/3 cup olive oil
- 1/3 cup white wine vinegar
- juice and zest of one half lemon
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 3/4 cup cup crumbled feta
- 1/2 cup fresh chopped dill

### Method
1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the orzo and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes. (Refer to package instructions). Drain and set aside.If you're using gluten-free orzo, I find it's helpful to run under cold water, then put back in the pot and drizzle with some olive oil to prevent sticking.
2. Add the orzo to a large bowl and toss with the olive oil, white wine vinegar, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and garlic powder.
3. Add the asparagus, zucchini, shallot, feta, and dill. Toss to evenly combine.
4. Serve immediately or keep chilled in the refrigerator until serving.
