### Ingredients
- 2 tbsp olive or avocado oil
- 1 medium yellow onion, finely chopped
- 4-6 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tsp salt + additional to taste
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 64oz crushed tomatoes
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 lb ground pork
- 1/4 cup finely chopped basil
- 1/2 cup gluten-free panko
- 1-2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- 2-4 tbsp avocado oil

### Method
1. In a large sauce pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until translucent and browning. Add the garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
2. Season the onion and garlic with the salt, pepper, red pepper flakes and Italian seasoning and cook for a few minutes, until fragrant.
3. Incorporate the tomato paste and cook down for 3-4 minutes. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, stir, and cover the pot. Cook for a least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally, on low heat.
4. In a large mixing bowl, mix together the eggs, beef, pork, basil, panko, and spices. Use clean hands to mix everything until fully incorporated. Use a cookie scoop or spoon to make ~2-inch meatballs and set aside (recipe makes about 16 meatballs).
5. Heat the avocado oil in a large skillet over medium heat, then add the meatballs (you may need to do multiple batches) and cook on all sides to brown and fully cook. Once cooked, carefully place the meatballs in the sauce and cover.
6. Let the sauce and meatballs simmer on low heat until serving. Top with additional fresh chopped basil if desired.
