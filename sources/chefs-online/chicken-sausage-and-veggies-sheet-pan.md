### Ingredients
- 1 medium head of broccoli, chopped into florets
- 1 pound brussels sprouts, halved
- 1 medium sweet potato, 1/4 to 1/2-inch dice
- 12 ounces pre-cooked chicken sausages, thinly sliced (4 links)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

### Method
1. Preheat the oven to 400ºF
2. Add the broccoli, brussels sprouts, sweet potato, and sliced chicken sausages to a large lined sheet pan.
3. Add the oil, smoked paprika, salt, and ground black pepper. With a spatula or clean hands, toss everything to evenly coat in the oil and spices.
4. Bake until the broccoli, brussels sprouts, and sweet potatoes are fork tender and lightly crisp, about 30 minutes.
