### Ingredients
- 1 1/2 pounds white fish of choice, haddock, halibut, pollock, or cod recommended
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 shallot, thinly sliced
- 1 lemon, 1/4-inch slices
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill (or 2 teaspoons dried)

### Method
1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Line a sheet pan with a piece of parchment about 1.5x the size needed for the sheet pan.
2. Combine the olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
3. Place the fish filets on the sheet pan and drizzle the olive oil mixture evenly over top of each.
4. Top each filet with even amount of sliced shallot, lemon slices, and chopped dill.
5. Wrap the fish in the parchment: fold up each side of the parchment covering as much of the fish as possible. (It’s okay if it’s not completely closed).
6. Bake until the fish reaches an internal temperature of 165F, about 15 minutes.
7. To serve, divide evenly among 4 plates, and top with a spoonful of the pan sauce. (serving suggestions included in notes)
