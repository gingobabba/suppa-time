### Ingredients
**Mole sauce:**
- low-sodium chicken broth: 3 cups
- chipotle peppers in adobo sauce: 2-4
- semi-sweet chocolate chips, heaping: 1/3 cup
- tomato paste, heaping: 1/3 cup
- roasted sesame seeds (or 1 tbsp tahini): 3 tablespoons
- creamy peanut butter: 3 tablespoons
- chili powder: 1 tablespoon
- cinnamon: 2 teaspoons
- garlic powder: 1 1/2 teaspoons
- kosher salt: 1 1/2 teaspoons
- cumin: 1/2 teaspoon

**Enchilada filling:**
- olive oil: 1 tablespoon
- small white onion, thinly sliced: 1/2
- bell pepper, thinly sliced: 1
- boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced: 1 1/2 pounds
- kosher salt: to taste
- large tortillas (flour or corn): 8-10
- shredded Monterey Jack or Mexican blend cheese: 2 cups

**Lime crema:**
- sour cream: 1/2 cup
- zest and juice of 1 lime: 1 lime
- water, as needed
- roasted sesame seeds, for garnish

### Notes
Vegetarian: swap chicken for sliced mushrooms or black beans. Dairy-free: omit cheese, use DF sour cream. Gluten-free: use corn tortillas.
