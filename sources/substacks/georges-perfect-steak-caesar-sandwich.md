### Ingredients
**Caesar aioli + dressing:**
- mayonnaise: 1 1/4 cups
- small garlic cloves, grated: 2
- lemon, zested (juiced later): 1
- Parmesan, finely grated: 1/4 cup
- Dijon mustard: 1 tablespoon
- Worcestershire sauce: 1 tablespoon
- black pepper: 1/2 teaspoon
- anchovy fillets packed in oil, finely chopped: 3

**Sandwiches:**
- New York strip steak (2-3 one-inch-thick steaks): 2 pounds
- kosher salt and black pepper: to taste
- large focaccia loaf, cut into 4 pieces: 1
- large romaine hearts, thinly sliced: 2
- head of radicchio, cored and thinly sliced (half used): 1
- olive oil: 2 tablespoons
- garlic cloves, smashed: 3
- butter: 3 tablespoons
- Parmesan, grated, divided: 1/2 cup
- pre-sliced pepperoncini, drained: 1/2 cup
- French fried onions: 2 cups

### Notes
Vegetarian: pan-seared cauliflower or portobello steaks in place of beef, vegan Worcestershire, swap anchovy for miso. Dairy-free: vegan Parm and DF butter. Great way to use up leftover grilled steak.
