### Ingredients
- bell pepper, very thinly sliced: 1
- medium zucchini, very thinly sliced into rounds: 1
- low-sodium vegetable or chicken stock: 2 1/2 cups
- jar salsa verde: 1 (12-ounce)
- white rice: 1 1/2 cups
- pinto beans, drained and rinsed: 1 (14-ounce) can
- white corn, drained and rinsed: 1 (15-ounce) can
- taco seasoning, divided: 2 tablespoons plus 2 teaspoons
- kosher salt: to taste
- peeled and deveined shrimp (16-20 per pound): 1 1/2 pounds
- extra-virgin olive oil: 1 tablespoon
- shredded Mexican cheese: 1 1/2 cups
- optional toppings: pico de gallo, pickled red onions, diced red onion, chopped cilantro, sour cream, hot sauce

### Notes
Vegetarian: skip the shrimp and double the beans. Dairy-free: skip the cheese or use a DF shredded cheese.
