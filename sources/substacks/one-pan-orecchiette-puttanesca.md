### Ingredients
- olive oil, plus 2 tablespoons for garnish: ¼ cup
- garlic cloves, crushed: 6
- chickpeas, drained and patted dry: 1 (15 oz) can
- hot smoked paprika (or any smoked paprika): 2 teaspoons
- ground cumin: 2 teaspoons
- tomato paste: ¾ tablespoon
- fresh parsley, roughly chopped: ⅔ cup
- lemon zest (from 1 medium lemon): 2 teaspoons
- baby capers (non-pareil): 3 tablespoons
- pitted green olives (e.g. Nocellara or Castelvetrano), roughly chopped: ½ cup
- small sweet cherry tomatoes: 1½ cups
- caster or granulated sugar: 2 teaspoons
- caraway seeds, lightly toasted and crushed: ½ tablespoon
- dried orecchiette: 8 oz
- vegetable or chicken stock: 2 cups
- water: ¾ cup
- kosher salt and black pepper: to taste

### Method
1. Add olive oil, garlic, chickpeas, smoked paprika, cumin, tomato paste, and ½ teaspoon salt to a large sauté pan over medium-high heat. Cook 12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until chickpeas are slightly crisp. (Lower heat if they colour too quickly.) Remove ⅓ of the chickpeas and set aside for garnish.
2. While chickpeas cook, combine parsley, lemon zest, capers, and olives in a small bowl. If caraway seeds aren't pre-toasted, toast in a dry skillet over medium heat 5 minutes until fragrant, then crush with a rolling pin or mortar and pestle.
3. To the pan with the remaining chickpeas, add ⅔ of the parsley mixture, cherry tomatoes, sugar, and crushed caraway seeds. Cook 2 minutes over medium-high, stirring often.
4. Add orecchiette, stock, ¾ teaspoon salt, and water. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat to medium, cover, and cook 12–14 minutes until pasta is al dente. (If liquid is gone but pasta isn't done, add a splash more water and cook covered another 1–2 minutes. If liquid remains but pasta is ready, remove lid, crank heat to high, and stir constantly until liquid evaporates.)
5. Stir in remaining parsley mixture. Drizzle with the extra 2 tablespoons olive oil and top with reserved crispy chickpeas and a grind of black pepper.

### Notes

One-pan pasta — everything cooks together. Serves 4.
