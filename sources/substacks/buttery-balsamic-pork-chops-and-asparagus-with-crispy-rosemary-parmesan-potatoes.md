### Ingredients
**Crispy rosemary Parmesan potatoes:**
- Yukon Gold potatoes, cut into 1/2-inch cubes: 2 pounds
- minced fresh rosemary: 1 1/2 tablespoons
- olive oil: 2 tablespoons
- kosher salt: 1 teaspoon
- freshly ground black pepper: 1/2 teaspoon
- garlic powder: 1/2 teaspoon
- Parmesan cheese, for grating: 1 block

**Pork chops + asparagus:**
- bone-in pork chops: 2
- olive oil: 1 tablespoon
- kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper: to taste
- unsalted butter, divided: 3 tablespoons
- rosemary sprig: 1
- asparagus, cut into 1-inch pieces on the bias: 1 bunch
- balsamic vinegar: 2 tablespoons
- honey: 3 teaspoons

**Lemon garlic aioli:**
- mayonnaise: 1/2 cup
- zest and juice of 1 lemon: 1 lemon
- garlic powder: 1/2 teaspoon
- kosher salt: 1/4 teaspoon
- Parmesan cheese, grated: 2 tablespoons

### Notes
Vegetarian: sautéed baby bella mushrooms in place of pork chops. Dairy-free: DF Parm or omit, DF butter or olive oil.
