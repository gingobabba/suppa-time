### Ingredients
- neutral oil (such as canola oil): as needed
- boneless, skinless chicken thighs: 2 pounds
- Brussels sprouts: 1 pound
- cornstarch: 3 tablespoons
- kosher salt: 1/2 teaspoon
- fresh orange juice: 3/4 cup
- sambal oelek, divided: 1-2 tablespoons
- low-sodium soy sauce: 3 tablespoons
- rice vinegar: 2 tablespoons
- fish sauce: 1 tablespoon
- scallions: 2
- toasted sesame seeds, for serving

### Notes
Vegetarian: omit chicken, double the veg, or use tofu/cauliflower. Dairy-free as written. Gluten-free: swap soy sauce for coconut aminos or tamari.
