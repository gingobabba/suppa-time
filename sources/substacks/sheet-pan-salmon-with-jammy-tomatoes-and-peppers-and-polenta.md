### Ingredients
**Sheet-pan salmon, tomatoes, and peppers:**
- orange bell pepper, thinly sliced: 1
- red bell pepper, thinly sliced: 1
- garlic cloves, smashed: 2
- lemon, cut in half: 1
- cherry or grape tomatoes: 1 pound
- olive oil, plus more for drizzling: 3 tablespoons
- kosher salt: 1 teaspoon
- freshly ground black pepper: to taste
- smoked paprika: 1/2 teaspoon
- salmon fillets (6-ounce): 4
- fresh basil leaves, sliced into ribbons: 1 small bunch
- unsalted butter: 2 tablespoons

**Creamy polenta:**
- low-sodium chicken broth: 4 cups
- quick-cooking polenta: 1 cup
- unsalted butter: 3 tablespoons
- sharp white Cheddar cheese, grated: 1 cup
- kosher salt: 1 teaspoon

### Notes
Vegetarian: swap salmon for a can or two of drained, rinsed chickpeas roasted with the veg. Dairy-free: DF cheddar and plant butter in the polenta, or skip cheese/polenta and serve with rice.
