### Ingredients
**Steak + marinade/dressing:**
- garlic clove, finely grated: 1 small
- small lemon, zested and juiced: 1
- fresh parsley, finely chopped: 1/4 cup
- fresh dill, finely chopped: 2 tablespoons
- extra-virgin olive oil: 3/4 cup
- red wine vinegar: 3 tablespoons
- Dijon mustard: 2 teaspoons
- dried oregano: 1 teaspoon
- kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper: to taste
- skirt (or flank or hanger) steak: 2 pounds

**Greek feta bruschetta:**
- small red onion, finely chopped: 1/4
- cherry tomatoes, cut into eighths: 1 cup
- English cucumber (or 4 Persian cucumbers), finely chopped: 1/2
- red bell pepper, finely chopped: 1
- Castelvetrano olives, finely chopped: 1 cup
- kosher salt: to taste
- feta, crumbled: 4 ounces

**For serving:**
- loaf good bread (sourdough or similar), thickly sliced: 1
- extra-virgin olive oil: for drizzling

### Notes
Vegetarian: marinated pan-fried or grilled tofu in place of steak. Dairy-free: DF feta or top with avocado instead.
