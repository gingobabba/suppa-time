### Ingredients
- ground chicken or turkey: 1 pound
- large egg: 1
- sharp cheddar cheese, freshly grated, divided: 2 cups
- panko breadcrumbs: 1/2 cup
- BBQ sauce, plus more for serving: 1/4 cup
- garlic powder, divided: 1 teaspoon
- kosher salt: to taste
- extra-virgin olive oil: as needed
- broccoli florets: 1 pound
- cherry tomatoes: 1 pint
- baby spinach, finely chopped: 2 cups
- unsalted butter: 2 tablespoons
- frozen white rice: 2 (10-ounce) bags
- whole milk: 1/4 cup
- freshly ground black pepper: to taste

### Notes
Vegetarian: use a plant-based ground meat. Dairy-free: DF shredded cheddar and DF milk/butter. Freezer-friendly — extra meatballs freeze well raw or cooked.
