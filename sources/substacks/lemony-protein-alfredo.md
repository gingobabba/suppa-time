### Ingredients
- kosher salt: to taste
- spaghetti or another long pasta (fettuccine, linguine): 1 pound
- lemon, zested and juiced: 1
- whole-milk cottage cheese: 1 cup
- Parmesan cheese, grated, plus more for serving: 1 cup
- garlic clove, smashed and peeled: 1
- unsalted butter, cut into chunks: 4 tablespoons
- ground black pepper: to taste

### Notes
Vegetarian. For extra protein, top with grilled or baked chicken or shrimp. Dairy-free: silken tofu can sub for the cottage-cheese sauce base.
