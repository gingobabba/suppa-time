### Ingredients
**Honey harissa chicken:**
- boneless, skinless chicken thighs: 2 pounds
- extra-virgin olive oil: 2 tablespoons
- harissa: 2 tablespoons
- honey: 2 tablespoons
- kosher salt: 1 1/2 teaspoons
- ground cumin: 1/2 teaspoon
- smoked paprika: 1/2 teaspoon

**Fancy rice:**
- unsalted butter: 2 tablespoons
- smoked paprika: 1/4 teaspoon
- turmeric: 1/4 teaspoon
- ground cumin: 1/4 teaspoon
- ground cinnamon: 1/8 teaspoon
- white rice: 2 cups
- low-sodium chicken stock: 3 cups
- juice of 1/2 lemon: 1/2 lemon
- honey: 1 tablespoon
- dried currants: 1/2 cup
- scallions, thinly sliced: 3
- green olives, finely chopped: 1/2 cup
- soft green herbs, finely chopped: 1/2 cup
- feta, crumbled: 4 ounces

**Lemon honey pickled red onions:**
- small red onion, thinly sliced: 1
- juice of 2 large lemons: 2 lemons
- honey: 1 tablespoon
- kosher salt: 1/4 teaspoon

**Harissa labneh:**
- labneh: 1 cup
- harissa: 2-3 teaspoons
- juice of 1/2 lemon: 1/2 lemon
- kosher salt: 1/4 teaspoon

### Notes
Vegetarian: swap chicken for pressed cubed tofu, chickpeas/white beans, or thick-sliced cauliflower or carrots. Dairy-free: omit feta or use DF feta, DF butter, and DF sour cream/hummus in place of labneh.
