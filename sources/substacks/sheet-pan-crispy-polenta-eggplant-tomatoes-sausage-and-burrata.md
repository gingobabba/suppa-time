### Ingredients
- globe eggplant, zebra-peeled, sliced into 1-inch pieces: 1
- precooked polenta (1-pound tube), cut into 1-inch pieces: 1
- garlic cloves, thinly sliced: 4
- extra-virgin olive oil: 1/4 cup
- cherry tomatoes: 1 pint
- thyme sprigs: 4 large
- kosher salt: 1 1/2 teaspoons
- Calabrian chili paste (or 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes): 2 teaspoons (up to 1 tablespoon)
- mild or spicy Italian sausage, uncooked, in casings: 1 pound
- Parmesan cheese, grated: 1/4 cup
- burrata, room temperature: 1 container

### Notes
Vegetarian sub: Impossible ground sausage, mushrooms, or drained white beans (add extra oil to compensate). Dairy-free: swap Parm/burrata for DF alternatives.
