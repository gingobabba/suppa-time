### Ingredients
**Creamy Chili Crisp Tahini Sauce:**
- tahini: 1/4 cup
- rice vinegar: 1 tablespoon
- low-sodium soy sauce: 1 tablespoon
- honey: 1 tablespoon
- chili crisp (crisp part only): 1 teaspoon

**Pork and Asparagus:**
- scallions, thinly sliced, divided: 1 bunch
- neutral oil (such as canola oil): 1 tablespoon
- fresh ginger, grated: 2-inch piece
- garlic cloves, grated: 3
- ground pork: 1 pound
- asparagus, woody ends trimmed, chopped: 1 bunch (about 1 pound)
- low-sodium soy sauce: 3 tablespoons
- rice vinegar: 3 tablespoons
- honey: 1 tablespoon
- chili crisp (crisp part only): 1 teaspoon

**For serving:**
- dry white or brown rice, cooked: 1 1/2 cups
- toasted sesame seeds, for garnish

### Notes
Vegetarian: plant-based ground meat, chopped mushrooms, or crumbled tofu. Dairy-free as written. Gluten-free: swap soy sauce for coconut aminos.
