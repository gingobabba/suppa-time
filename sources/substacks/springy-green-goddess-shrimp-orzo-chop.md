### Ingredients
**Orzo and shrimp:**
- kosher salt: to taste
- U-20 raw shrimp, peeled and deveined: 1 1/2 pounds
- freshly ground black pepper: to taste
- dry orzo: 1 cup
- extra-virgin olive oil: as needed
- juice of 1 lemon: 1 lemon

**Green Goddess Dressing:**
- fresh parsley: 1 packed cup
- fresh dill: 1/2 packed cup
- chives, roughly chopped: 1/4 cup
- Greek yogurt: 1/2 cup
- small garlic clove: 1
- juice of 1 medium lemon: 1 lemon
- extra-virgin olive oil: 3 tablespoons
- Dijon mustard: 1 tablespoon
- Worcestershire sauce: 1 teaspoon
- honey: 1 teaspoon
- kosher salt: 1/2 teaspoon

**Salad:**
- romaine hearts, chopped: 2
- pre-shredded cabbage or slaw mix: 1 (10-12 ounce) bag
- snap peas, thinly sliced: 1 cup
- English cucumber, chopped: 1/2
- scallions, thinly sliced: 3
- large avocado, diced: 1
- feta, crumbled: 4-6 ounces
