### Ingredients
- kosher salt: to taste
- dried spaghetti or any long noodle: 1 pound
- extra-virgin olive oil: 1/2 cup
- large egg: 1
- Parmigiano-Reggiano, freshly grated, plus more for garnish: 1 cup
- lemon zest (about 1 tsp), plus more for garnish: zest of 1 lemon
- lemon juice: 1/3 cup (from 2–3 lemons)
- freshly ground black pepper: 1/4 teaspoon
- large basil leaves, thinly sliced: 12 (or 6 parsley sprigs)

### Method
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta until al dente per package instructions. Before draining, reserve 1 cup of pasta cooking water. Drain.
2. Meanwhile, whisk together olive oil, egg, Parmesan, lemon zest, lemon juice, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and pepper in a large serving bowl.
3. Thinly slice the basil.
4. Immediately add the hot drained pasta to the sauce along with 1/2 cup of the hot reserved pasta water and most of the basil. Toss continuously until the sauce is glossy and coats the noodles — it will look soupy at first, keep tossing. The cheese, oil, and egg need heat and time to come together. Add more pasta water if the sauce tightens up.
5. Serve immediately garnished with more Parmesan, lemon zest, and basil.
