### Ingredients
- honey: 1/4 cup
- neutral cooking oil: 1/4 cup
- ground cumin: 1 teaspoon
- paprika: 1 teaspoon
- cayenne pepper: 1/8 teaspoon
- garlic cloves, minced: 4
- kosher salt: 2 teaspoons
- bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs: 1 1/2 to 2 pounds
- carrots, cut into 2-inch pieces on the bias: 1 pound
- brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved: 1 pound
- cilantro or parsley, finely minced: 1/3 cup

**Tahini yogurt:**
- tahini: 1/4 cup
- lemon juice: juice of 1 lemon
- Greek yogurt: 1 cup
- garlic clove, minced (or 1/4 tsp garlic powder): 1
- kosher salt and pepper: to taste

### Method
1. Preheat oven to 425°F and line a baking sheet with parchment.
2. Whisk honey, oil, cumin, paprika, cayenne, garlic, and salt in a large bowl.
3. Add chicken, carrots, and brussels sprouts to the baking sheet. Pour marinade over and toss to coat. Spread into a single layer — chicken skin-side up, brussels cut-side down.
4. Roast on the centre rack for 40 minutes. Stir the veg halfway through if desired. If the chicken skin starts to burn, cover loosely with foil.
5. Meanwhile, whisk tahini and lemon juice until smooth — add a splash of hot water if needed to work out the lumps. Stir in Greek yogurt, garlic, and a big pinch of salt and pepper. Taste and adjust, adding more lemon for a tangier sauce.
6. Stir cilantro or parsley into the chicken and veg. Serve with tahini yogurt.
