### Ingredients
**Salad:**
- large bunch lacinato kale (about 10 ounces), thinly sliced: 1
- kosher salt: to taste
- celery stalks, thinly sliced: 3
- pitted Castelvetrano olives, drained and chopped: 5-ounce jar
- dill fronds and tender stems, thinly sliced: 1/2 cup
- fresh mint leaves, thinly sliced: 1/2 cup
- scallions, thinly sliced: 1 bunch
- optional: dates, chopped, and apple, thinly sliced: 4 dates, 1 apple
- pre-cooked lentils: 16 ounces
- feta, crumbled: 8 ounces
- roasted pistachios, chopped: 1/2 cup
- juice of 1 lemon: 1 lemon

**Lemon Masala Dressing:**
- extra-virgin olive oil: 1/2 cup
- zest and juice of 1 lemon: 1 lemon
- apple cider vinegar: 2 tablespoons
- garam masala: 1/2 teaspoon
- ground cumin: 1/2 teaspoon
- Dijon mustard: 2 teaspoons
- honey: 2 teaspoons
- kosher salt: 1 teaspoon

### Notes
No-cook if using pre-cooked refrigerated lentils. Dairy-free: use DF feta or omit. Add pan-seared shrimp, rotisserie chicken, salmon, or grilled steak for more protein.
