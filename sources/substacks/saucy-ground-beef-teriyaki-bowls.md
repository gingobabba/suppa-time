### Ingredients

**Teriyaki sauce:**
- low-sodium soy sauce or coconut aminos: 1/2 cup
- cornstarch, arrowroot, or tapioca powder: 1 tablespoon
- water: 1/2 cup
- sesame oil: 2 tablespoons
- coconut sugar: 1/4 cup
- garlic powder: 1 teaspoon
- ground ginger: 1 teaspoon

**Beef bowls:**
- small onion, finely diced: 1
- garlic cloves, mashed: 3
- red bell pepper, diced: 1
- broccoli florets, finely diced: 1 1/2 cups
- shredded carrots: 3/4 cup
- olive oil: 2 tablespoons
- ground beef (90/10): 1 pound
- jasmine rice, cooked per package instructions: 1 cup
- garnishes: sliced green onions, sesame seeds

### Method
1. Cook rice per package instructions. Remove from heat and keep covered.
2. Make the teriyaki sauce: whisk soy sauce and cornstarch together until fully dissolved. Add water, sesame oil, coconut sugar, garlic powder, and ginger and whisk until combined. Set aside.
3. Prep all veg and keep them separate — they go in at different times.
4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and sauté for 4 minutes until soft. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute. Add ground beef and cook for about 4 minutes, breaking into small pieces.
5. Add bell pepper, broccoli, and carrots. Cook until beef is cooked through and veg has softened.
6. Pour teriyaki sauce over and stir for a few minutes until thickened.
7. Fluff rice and assemble bowls. Spoon extra sauce over the top and garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.

### Notes
Avocado oil substituted with olive oil.
