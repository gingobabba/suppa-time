### Ingredients
- short pasta (farfalle, fusilli, gemelli, or cavatappi): 16 ounces
- kosher salt: to taste
- fresh corn, kernels cut off (or canned/thawed frozen): 6 ears
- scallions, thinly sliced: 6
- sesame oil: 1/3 cup
- sour cream: 1/3 cup
- white miso paste: 1/3 cup
- rice vinegar: 1/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon
- zest and juice of 2 medium limes: 2 limes
- hot honey (or honey plus sriracha/gochujang): 1 tablespoon
- cilantro, leaves and tender stems, chopped: 1 bunch
- toasted sesame seeds: 2 tablespoons
- flaky salt, to finish: 1 teaspoon
- optional: crispy cooked bacon, chopped: 1 pound
- optional garnish: French fried onions

### Notes
Vegetarian as written. Dairy-free: swap sour cream for DF sour cream or mayo. Add shredded rotisserie chicken or white beans for more protein.
