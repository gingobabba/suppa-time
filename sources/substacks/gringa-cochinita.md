### Ingredients
- boneless pork butt, cut into 6 chunks: 3 pounds
- taco seasoning: 2 tablespoons
- kosher salt, divided: 1 1/2 teaspoons
- garlic powder: 1 teaspoon
- ground cinnamon: 1/8 teaspoon
- ground allspice: 1/8 teaspoon
- ground cloves: 1/8 teaspoon
- white onion, roughly chopped: 1
- ripe tomatoes, roughly chopped: 2
- grapefruit: 1 large
- oranges: 2 large
- limes, divided: 7
- large red onion, diced: 1
- habanero pepper, cut into 4 pieces (optional): 1
- sugar: 1/2 teaspoon
- tortillas: 1 package
- optional toppings: cilantro, sour cream, cheese

### Notes
Mostly hands-off — 30 min active, up to 3.5 hours in the oven. Dairy-free as written. Gluten-free with corn or grain-free tortillas. Vegetarian: try jackfruit in place of pork.
