### Ingredients
- garlic cloves, grated: 2
- fresh ginger, grated: 2-inch piece
- soft green herbs (oregano, mint, parsley, basil, cilantro), finely chopped: 1/2 cup
- extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for oiling lemons: 3 tablespoons
- Dijon mustard: 1 tablespoon
- lemons, divided: 4
- honey: 2 tablespoons
- kosher salt: 3 teaspoons
- boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces: 3 pounds
- neutral oil (such as canola oil), for oiling the grill grates: as needed

### Notes
From a larger dinner-party menu — pairs well with a grain and a salad for a weeknight version. Vegetarian: swap chicken for mixed vegetable skewers (zucchini, squash, bell pepper, red onion, mushroom) marinated the same way. No grill: broil 5-6 inches from heat, 5 minutes per side.
