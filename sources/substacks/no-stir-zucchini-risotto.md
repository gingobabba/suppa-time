### Ingredients
- reduced-sodium chicken or vegetable stock (or 4 cups water + 4 tsp Better Than Bouillon): 4 cups
- shallots, minced: 2
- garlic cloves, minced: 4
- zucchini (medium), grated: 3
- arborio rice (do not rinse): 2 cups
- Parmesan cheese, grated (ideally Parmigiano Reggiano), plus more for garnish: 1 cup
- butter: 4 tablespoons
- large lemon, halved: 1
- kosher salt and black pepper: to taste
- fresh basil, thinly sliced (optional): for garnish

### Method
1. Preheat oven to 350°F with a rack in the middle.
2. Bring stock to a boil in a large oven-proof heavy-bottomed pot or Dutch oven over high heat. Meanwhile, mince shallots and garlic and grate zucchini.
3. Turn off the heat and stir in the arborio rice, grated zucchini, shallots, and garlic. Cover (a baking sheet works if the pot has no lid) and transfer to the oven. Bake for 40 minutes.
4. Remove from the oven and stir in Parmesan, butter, and the zest and juice of half the lemon. Season with salt and pepper. If too thick, add a splash of stock to loosen. If too thin, let sit a minute or two — the rice will keep absorbing liquid.
5. Serve as-is or topped with any protein. Garnish with extra Parmesan, lemon zest, and basil.

### Notes
Serves 6 — very filling as a vegetarian main.
